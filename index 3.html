🎤 The Power of Words - TED Talk

Transcript
Words are powerful tools. They can inspire us to achieve great things, or they can damage our spirits. The way we communicate with others shapes our relationships and defines who we are. Every day, we have the opportunity to choose our words carefully. A kind word can brighten someone's day, while a harsh comment can leave lasting scars. Research shows that positive language not only improves our mental health but also boosts our immune system. In the digital age, written communication has become even more important. Before sending a message, let's pause and consider: Would I say this in person? If not, perhaps we should rethink our approach. Let's use words to build bridges, not walls.
